## Technical Details (For Reference)

### What Gets Scraped:
1. **Grants & Subsidies**: Government funding, EU grants, green shipping subsidies
2. **Legal Violations**: Environmental fines, lawsuits, regulatory violations
3. **Sustainability News**: Wind propulsion, green technology, decarbonization
4. **Reputation**: Sustainability ratings, environmental performance scores
5. **Financial Pressure**: Carbon tax exposure, EU ETS costs, compliance costs

### How It Works:
- Uses **Google Gemini 2.0 Flash** (free tier)
- Sends structured prompts with company name
- Gemini searches web and returns JSON
- Each finding includes: title, snippet, URL, date, source
- Rate limited: 30 seconds between requests
- Saves to `company_intelligence_gemini_*.json`

### Data Sources:
- Maritime news: Lloyd's List, TradeWinds, Splash247
- Government sites: EU, German, UK ministries
- Legal databases: Court records, regulatory filings
- Industry ratings: CDP, RightShip, sustainability rankings
